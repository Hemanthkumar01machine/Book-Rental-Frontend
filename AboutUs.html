Our Story

It all began with a small dream......

To unite all the bibliophiles out their and bring them together. So that they can share their combined love
for books. With this cause we made bibliophilia, a utopia for book readers. Here, people can not only find people similar to them but also share books in the largest community of bibliophiles. A renting platform where people can instead of buying, rent their favourite books and put up their own collection for others to rent.
